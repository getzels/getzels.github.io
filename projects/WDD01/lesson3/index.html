Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the plan of the getzelsweather.com website, to define what its audience is and the purpose for which said audience would be visiting the page and what information they could get, also to define its structure and style. and that it serves as a blueprint for its development. Define the different services that the website will provide, such as real-time weather information, articles on weather-related topics.

Target Audience and Scenarios

The audience for this web page is people who are interested in wheate information, but not only the real time weather information but general information about weather and serve as a weather site communite, where people can share information. The age of the audience are people from 15 years old and older. Persona in general who want to know about the weather and studen who are interested in information about how the weather works. Person 1: Nicolas de Los Santos, men of 32 years old, father software developer, father of the kids, one of them play baseball, is instrested in know the weather information . Person 2: Kenia Cabrera, woman, of 24 years all, student and is often looking for information about weather to do some assignment for the university.
